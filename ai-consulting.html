The automations we run at District Angels

Everything below runs our own community day to day. It is also the menu we build from for other groups.

Deal intake to pipelineFounder submissions flow from our intake form into a structured deal database, deduped and tagged on arrival.
First-pass screeningEvery inbound deal is scored against our thesis and focus sectors before anyone on the team spends time on it.
Five-day response loopFounders get an acknowledgment on submit and a tracked nudge, so nobody waits past five days for next steps.
Memo draftingDiligence notes turn into a structured investment-memo draft that our Fellows and team sharpen.
Market report supportFellows get research scaffolding and formatting so each market report ships clean under their own name.
Angel matchingMembers are matched to deals and to each other by interest, background, and seniority.
Deal room prepEach monthly session auto-compiles the pitch, memo, and Q&A into one packet for the room.
Member onboardingNew members get a welcome, the right Slack access, and the channels that fit them, with no manual setup.
SPV and commitment trackingSoft commitments and SPV allocations are tracked from the deal room through to close.
