Thesis

The talent is here. The capital just sits still

Washington holds the research, the institutions, and some of the highest-income counties in the country. Very little of it reaches early-stage founders. We're the connective tissue that moves it.

The cost of building drops every quarter, and more of the value now gets created at the earliest stage, by the people who show up first. The money to back them already lives here. What's missing is a way for everyday professionals to put it to work.

So we built one. Three things keep good capital on the sidelines, and each is something we set out to fix:

No on-rampNew and aspiring angels have nowhere obvious to start.→ We're the on-ramp: a monthly deal room, a hands-on fellowship, and a community you can actually join.
Diligence is hardDoing it well takes time and expertise most people don't have spare.→ We share the work: open memos, deal screens, and market reports anyone can build on.
Groups stay closedExisting angel groups can feel siloed and hard to break into.→ We stay open and co-invest instead of gatekeeping.

For investors

However you show up, there's a way in

Curated deal flow, sharp diligence, and a room full of people who take building seriously. Wherever you are on the curve, you'll find your level.

Never invested

Start without the guesswork

You don't need a track record to belong. Professionals who've never written a check start here: see live deals at the deal room, learn the moves through the fellowship, and get real exposure to innovation while backing the ecosystem you live in.

Experienced angel

Sharper signal, better company

Curated, diligence-backed opportunities, co-investment alongside credible leads, and invitation-only gatherings with people operating at your altitude. Less noise, more conviction.

Just curious

Come see how it works

Pull up to the monthly deal room, meet the community, and decide for yourself. No commitment to walk in the door.

International corridor

The capital region's global access, put to work

The region runs on 170+ embassies and the world's largest multilateral institutions. No other community has this on its doorstep. Here's how we turn it into capital and connection:

  • / Train foreign nationals and embassy networks to invest as angels in US startups
  • / Host private events that put institutions and investors in front of companies founded by diaspora founders in the US
  • / Help international and diaspora founders raise from angels who understand their markets
