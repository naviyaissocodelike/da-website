Thesis

Angel investing here is harder than it should be

No on-rampNew and aspiring angels have nowhere obvious to start
Diligence is hardDoing it well takes time and expertise most people don't have spare
Groups stay closedExisting angel groups can feel siloed and hard to break into
Our belief

The capital region has the talent, the institutions, and some of the highest-income counties in the country, but barely any of that money reaches the founders building here. At the same time, starting a company has never been cheaper, so the people who back founders early matter more than ever. The money is already here, and most people just never had a way to invest it. We built one.

Our approach
  • /
    Free to join, but curated. We match angels by interest, background, and seniority, so the right people end up in the room together
  • /
    Industry-agnostic, with a special affinity for:
    Dual-use & defenseCyberBiotechHealth techLife sciencesClimateFintech & crypto
  • /
    Checks start at $2.5K per person to keep investing accessible. Total investment per deal, pooled into an SPV, runs from $50K to $200K
  • /
    Open to co-investing alongside other angel groups

International corridor

170+ embassies on our doorstep

The region runs on 170+ embassies and the world's largest multilateral institutions. No other community has this on its doorstep. Here's how we turn it into capital and connection:

  • / Train foreign nationals and embassy networks to invest as angels in US startups
  • / Host private events that put institutions and investors in front of companies founded by diaspora founders in the US
  • / Help international and diaspora founders raise from angels who understand their markets
